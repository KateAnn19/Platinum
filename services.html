What We Do

We provide professionally trained and licensed bar staff as well as high quality service staff for hire at all types of Tulsa events and weddings. Our bartenders have knowledge of how to make drinks and the ingredients surrounding those specialty drinks that every event wants to provide. All bartenders bring their own bar tools in order to make your bar event successful. All staff are required to have a food handler card to be in compliance with our Tulsa Health Department as well as a liquor license provided by the Oklahoma Able Commission.

The following is what our staff can provide at time of event

  • Setup of tables, chairs, and equipment
  • Setup of table settings
  • Passed appetizers & cocktails
  • Buffet setup & Service
  • Bar setup
  • Plated dinners
  • Clearing of tables at end of event
  • Breakdown of event at end of service
